Buckwheat with Meat

Description

Easy-to-make high-protein dinner. It doesn't require extreme effort, but it makes you stay in the kitchen for a while. Back in the days it was hard to find buckwheat in any sort of stores but nowadays this is not a problem so you should find all the ingredients at your local store or in the big supermarkets, depending on where do you live.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Buckwheat
  • Meat (usually chicken)
  • Water
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Salt and your favourite seasonings

Steps

  1. Cut the onion and the carrot into cubes
  2. Cut the meat into pieces
  3. Fry the meat in the oil for a while, then add the onion and the carrot
  4. Add the seasonings
  5. Add the canned tomatoes and fry for 5 minutes
  6. Add the buckwheat and the water in proportion 1:3 (3 times more water than buckwheat)
  7. Cook them until the boiling point, then lower the heat
  8. Cook until the water is evaporated
  9. Let it cool down for 5 minutes and VOILA, you can serve your dinner
